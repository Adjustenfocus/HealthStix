# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 13 JULY 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how HealthStix ("the App," "we," "us") handles
information when you use our mobile application. HealthStix is developed and operated
by JUSTEN WILSON.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: **healthstixsupport@gmail.com**

---

## 1. Summary

HealthStix helps you track peptide protocols, doses, and progress — including for
multiple people in one household (e.g. a spouse or family member you help manage
shots for). Account and tracking data is stored using Supabase, a hosted database
provider, so it can sync across your devices and be shared within your household
account. **Progress photos are the one exception: they are stored only on your device
and are never uploaded anywhere.**

We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account information
When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed
password (we never store or see your plain-text password). This is used solely to
identify your account and let you sign in.

### 2.2 Health-adjacent tracking information
The App lets you record information related to peptide protocols for yourself and
anyone else in your household you manage, including: peptide/substance names,
vial and reconstitution details, doses, schedules, injection sites, administration
records, symptoms, and related notes ("Health-Adjacent Data").

**This data is stored using Supabase**, a third-party hosted database and
authentication provider. Supabase stores this data on our behalf so it can sync
across your devices and be shared among the profiles in your household account. We do
not sell this data, and we do not share it with advertisers or unrelated third
parties.

### 2.3 Sharing your data with another account (optional)
The App lets you optionally generate a time-limited invite code that grants another
person's separate HealthStix account **read-only** access to one specific profile's
protocol and dose history (what was taken, when, and how much). Symptoms, benefit
notes, and progress photos are never included in this shared view. You can revoke
this access at any time, which takes effect immediately. This is separate from
syncing your own data across your own devices (Section 2.2) — it involves a
different person's own separate account, not just your own devices.

### 2.4 Biometric authentication (optional)
If you enable fingerprint or Face ID unlock, this is handled entirely by your device's
operating system. **Your biometric data never leaves your device and is never sent to
us or to Supabase.** The App only receives a yes/no confirmation that authentication
succeeded.

### 2.5 Infrastructure and platform data
The App is built using Expo/EAS. Depending on which features are enabled, this can
include:
- **Push notification tokens**, if reminders are enabled, brokered through Expo's push
  notification service.
- **Crash and error diagnostic data**, if error-reporting is enabled, to help us fix
  bugs.

### 2.6 App store platform data
Apple and Google collect certain data independently of us as part of operating their
app stores and devices. This is governed by Apple's and Google's own privacy policies,
not this one.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above only to:
- Operate core App functionality (accounts, syncing your tracking data across your
  devices, and sharing it among the profiles in your household)
- Enable optional sign-in, authentication, and reminder features
- Diagnose and fix technical problems, if crash reporting is enabled

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell or rent it to any
third party.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with advertisers. The table below shows every party
that has access to any information, and what they receive:

| Data | Stored/Shared with | What's shared |
|---|---|---|
| Account email, tracking data (doses, protocols, logs) | Supabase (our hosted database provider) | Stored on their servers on our behalf, under their security and privacy terms |
| Progress photos | No one | Stays on your device only — never uploaded |
| Biometric unlock | No one | Nothing — handled on-device by iOS/Android |
| Push notifications (if enabled) | Expo | Device push token only |
| Crash reports (if enabled) | Expo / diagnostic provider | Technical error data |

Supabase acts as our data processor/subprocessor — they host the infrastructure but do
not use your data for their own purposes. You can review Supabase's own privacy and
security practices at supabase.com/privacy.

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your account and tracking data is retained for as long as your account is active. You
can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting
us at **healthstixsupport@gmail.com**; we will delete it from Supabase within 30 days. 
You can also delete your account and all associated data yourself at any time from 
within the App (Account & Settings > Delete Account) — this immediately erases everything 
on your device and in Supabase. Deleting your account this way does not remove your 
login credential itself; email us if you'd like that removed too.

Progress photos are stored only on your device, so deleting them or uninstalling the
App removes them immediately — we never had a copy to begin with.

used to help manage a household member's protocol, the account holder is responsible
